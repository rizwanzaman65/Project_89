EMIS is a three-dimensional, object-oriented modeling and management system - It enables designers to use objects, created and stored in library databases, to design, visualize, manage and operate various types of large engineering projects.  EMIS is not a CAD drafting system - Information management is the main goal of EMIS. Data driven graphics rather than graphics driven data is how information is managed.
